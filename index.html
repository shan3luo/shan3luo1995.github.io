Biography

I am currently a lecturer in the Department of Applied Statistics at Beijing Technology and Business University. Before joining BTBU, I obtained my PhD in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Peking University (2017.09 - 2022.07), supervised by Prof Yangbo He.

Research Interests

  • Causal inference: attribution analysis, covariate adjustment, instrumental variable, principal stratification, propensity score, spillover effect

  • Missing data: nonignorable missing data
